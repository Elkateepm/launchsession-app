Safeguarding & trust

Designed to support secure
and responsible data handling.

Youth data deserves real care. Every part of LaunchSession is built around who should — and shouldn't — see what.

🔐
Role-based permissions
Staff, volunteers and admins each see only what their role needs — nothing more.
🏢
Organisation-level separation
Every organisation's data is fully isolated. Nothing is ever shared across accounts.
🔑
Secure authentication
Industry-standard encrypted sign-in, with password reset and session management built in.
📜
Audit trails
Key actions on sensitive records are tracked, so you can see what changed and when.
🛡️
Safeguarding workflows
Concerns are logged, assigned and tracked through to resolution — never lost in email.
🔒
Sensitive record restrictions
Safeguarding and case management records are restricted to the people who need them.
