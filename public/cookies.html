Cookie Policy

LaunchSession is provided by LaunchNexus Ltd (company no. 17333693), 14 Souldern Street, Watford, United Kingdom, WD18 0EU

This policy explains how LaunchNexus Ltd uses cookies and similar technologies (such as local storage and session storage) on the LaunchSession marketing site (launchsession.co.uk) and the LaunchSession application (app.launchsession.co.uk).

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. "Similar technologies" includes browser local storage and session storage, which work in a comparable way but are not sent to the server automatically with every request.

Categories of cookies and storage we use

Strictly necessary

Required for the Service to function — for example, keeping you signed in (Supabase authentication session tokens), maintaining your selected organisation context, and remembering short-lived interface state (such as which tab you last viewed). These cannot be switched off, as the Service will not work without them.

Preferences

Used to remember your choices, such as dark mode preference or dismissed banners, so you don't have to reset them on every visit.

Analytics

We do not currently use analytics cookies or tools on the marketing site or the application. If this changes in future, this policy will be updated before any such technology is introduced.

Marketing

We do not use marketing or advertising cookies.

Managing cookies

Most browsers let you refuse or delete cookies through their settings. Blocking strictly necessary cookies will prevent sign-in and core functionality from working. You can also clear local/session storage via your browser's developer tools or settings.

Changes

We may update this policy as our use of cookies changes. Material changes will be reflected in the "last updated" date above.
