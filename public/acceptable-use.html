Acceptable Use Policy

LaunchSession is provided by LaunchNexus Ltd (company no. 17333693), 14 Souldern Street, Watford, United Kingdom, WD18 0EU

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") applies to all use of LaunchSession by any user of a Customer organisation's account, and is incorporated into the Terms of Service.

1. General conduct

You must not use the Service to:

  • upload, store, or transmit unlawful, defamatory, obscene, or infringing content;
  • attempt to gain unauthorised access to another organisation's data, accounts, or any part of the Service's infrastructure;
  • probe, scan, or test the vulnerability of the Service, or circumvent any security or authentication measure, without our prior written permission;
  • introduce viruses, malware, or other harmful code;
  • use the Service to send unsolicited communications (spam) or engage in phishing;
  • reverse-engineer, decompile, or attempt to extract the source code of the Service, except to the extent permitted by law;
  • resell, sublicense, or provide the Service to third parties outside your own organisation without our consent;
  • use automated means (bots, scrapers) to access the Service outside of documented integrations, without permission.

2. Safeguarding-specific expectations

Because LaunchSession is used to record safeguarding, welfare, and health-related information about children and vulnerable individuals, organisations and their users must:

  • only enter safeguarding or "cause for concern" records for genuine, good-faith safeguarding purposes, and in line with the organisation's own safeguarding policy;
  • restrict access to safeguarding modules to staff with an appropriate role and need to know, using the role-based permissions available in the Service;
  • not use the Service as a substitute for statutory reporting obligations — where a concern meets the threshold for referral to social services, the police, or another statutory body, the organisation must follow its own safeguarding procedures and legal duties in addition to any record kept in the Service;
  • ensure that any photos, media, or identifying information relating to children uploaded to the Service have appropriate consent from a parent/carer in line with the organisation's own policies.

3. Account security

Users must keep login credentials confidential, use a unique password, and notify their organisation's admin (who should in turn notify us) promptly if they suspect unauthorised access to their account.

4. Consequences of breach

We may suspend or terminate access for a user or an organisation's account where this policy is breached, following the process set out in the Terms of Service, except where immediate suspension is necessary to prevent harm, in which case we may act first and notify promptly afterwards.

5. Reporting misuse

If you become aware of a breach of this policy, please report it to info@launchnexus.co.uk.
